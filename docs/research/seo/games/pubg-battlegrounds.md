# PUBG Battlegrounds — SEO / GEO Research

## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global. Steam: **301,271 current / 782,305 daily peak** (Steam stats, 2026-05-11, Source: https://store.steampowered.com/stats/stats/?l=english). PC bang Korea: **7.69% of PC bang play time** (June 2025, Gametrics/KOCCA, Source: https://welcon.kocca.kr). Mobile (PUBG Mobile): **$1.12B 2025 revenue, 116M downloads** (AppMagic 2026, Source: https://appmagic.rocks/files/view/upload/Reports/EN_MobileMarkeLandscape2026.pdf). Developed by KRAFTON (South Korean company). Strong in Korea, SEA, EU. 

**Dominant languages:** Korean (developer HQ + PC bang), English, Chinese (mobile via PUBG Mobile). Japanese audience exists.

**Key communities:**
- Reddit: r/PUBG (~500K members), r/PUBGConsole
- Korea: Inven PUBG section, DCInside PUBG gallery — very active
- Red Bull: "The 10 types of PUBG player" article (cited in SERP, shows archetype demand)
- Steam community: "TYPES OF PLAYERS IN PUBG" guide (high views, shows archetype vocabulary demand)
- Discord: PUBG official, regional competitive servers

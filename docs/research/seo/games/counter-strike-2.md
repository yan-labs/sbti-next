# Counter-Strike 2 — SEO / GEO Research

---

## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global PC dominant. Steam current player count: **729,651 live / 1,405,012 daily peak** (Steam stats, 2026-05-11, Source: https://store.steampowered.com/stats/stats/?l=english). PC playtime share: **7.0% of all PC gaming** in 2025 (Newzoo, Source: https://investgame.net/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/2026-03-12-newzoo_2026_pc_and_console_gaming_report_wp.pdf). Twitch: **61.99M viewer hours** (May 2026, TwitchMetrics, Source: https://www.twitchmetrics.net/games/viewership). EU (HLTV, FACEIT) and CIS are strongest; Korea PC bangs show some signal but not top 10.

**Dominant languages:** English (primary), Russian, Portuguese (Brazilian scene), Chinese (CSGO had large CN audience). Korean audience is secondary but real (PC bangs).

**Key communities:**
- HLTV.org — the definitive CS stats/news site; all serious players visit
- FACEIT — competitive matchmaking; community pages
- Reddit: r/GlobalOffensive, r/cs2
- China: NGA CS section, Bilibili CS2 content
- Discord: FACEIT Hub servers, team servers
- Twitch: s1mple (even after retirement legend status), NiKo, ZywOo

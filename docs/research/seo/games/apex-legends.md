## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global. Steam: **111,821 current / 303,526 daily peak** (Steam stats, 2026-05-11, Source: https://store.steampowered.com/stats/stats/?l=english). Twitch: **15.3M viewer hours** (May 2026, TwitchMetrics, Source: https://www.twitchmetrics.net/games/viewership). EA/Respawn. Available on PC (Steam + EA App), console (PS/Xbox), Mobile (sunset 2023). NA-dominant with growing global footprint.

**Dominant languages:** English (NA/EU dominant), some Korean and Chinese.

**Key communities:**
- Reddit: r/apexlegends (~2.5M members); very meme-active
- Discord: Official Apex Discord, team servers
- Korea: Inven Apex section; smaller but real community
- China: Bilibili Apex content (limited due to CN server history)
- Twitch: ImperialHal, Verhulst, Genburten — top content creators
- Noted culture: "third-party" awareness is a meme; legend movement (sliding, bunny-hopping) is identity-forming

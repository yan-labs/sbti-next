## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global. PC bang Korea: **4.71% of PC bang play time** (June 2025, Gametrics/KOCCA, Source: https://welcon.kocca.kr). Twitch: **28.9M viewer hours** (May 2026, TwitchMetrics, Source: https://www.twitchmetrics.net/games/viewership). Free-to-play since OW2 launch (Oct 2022) significantly expanded the player base. Blizzard runs OWL (Overwatch League). NA, Korea, and EU are the three dominant markets.

**Dominant languages:** English (NA/EU), Korean, Chinese. Japanese audience is smaller but present.

**Key communities:**
- Reddit: r/Overwatch (~4M members), r/Competitiveoverwatch
- Discord: Official Overwatch Discord, team-specific servers
- Korea: Inven OW section, dcinside OW gallery
- China: Bilibili OW2 channels, NGA
- Twitch: Jay3, ML7, Awkward (pro/streamer crossover audience)
- YouTube: Blizzard's own patch breakdowns draw quiz-ready audiences

## 5. Competitor SERP Snapshot

**"overwatch 2 personality quiz"**
- HowStuffWorks: "Do You Main Tank, Support or DPS in Overwatch?" (role quiz, not player-type depth)
- BrainFall: "Which Overwatch Character Are You?" (character-based, stale)
- QuizExpo: "Which Overwatch Character Are You?" (20 questions, no structured data)
- Twinfinite: "Which Overwatch 2 DPS Character Are You?" (editorial, DPS-only)
- Source: WebSearch results (May 2026)

**Gap:** No competitor offers a player behavior archetype quiz (how do you *play*, not which hero matches you). SBTI's 8 archetypes based on Bond × Tempo × Mental axes fill a gap that character-matching quizzes leave open. "C9 trauma curator" and "DPS victim" are meme-vocabulary archetypes with no existing quiz equivalents.

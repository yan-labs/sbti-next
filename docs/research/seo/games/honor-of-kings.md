# Honor of Kings (王者荣耀) — SEO / GEO Research

---

## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Primarily Chinese-speaking (China, Taiwan, HK, SEA Chinese communities). **#1 global mobile game by revenue in 2025** ($1.68B) and **top grossing in April 2026** (Sensor Tower, Source: https://sensortower.com/blog/top-worldwide-mobile-game-revenue-april-2026). **62,196 attendees** at 2025 KPL Grand Finals — Guinness World Record for largest esports live attendance (Source: https://www.guinnessworldrecords.com/news/commercial/2025/11/62000-honor-of-kings-fans-make-history-as-largest-ever-audience-for-an-esports-match). Level Infinite's 2026 global restructuring (KML) expands into Indonesia, Philippines, Malaysia, Brazil, Korea, Japan, South Asia, MENA (Source: https://esportsinsider.com/2025/12/honor-of-kings-creates-new-global-esports-league-2026).

**Dominant languages:** Chinese (zh) is overwhelming primary. Growing: Indonesian, Portuguese (Brazil), Korean, Japanese.

**Key communities:**
- Official: WeChat mini-programs, QQ community, Weibo
- China: Bilibili (millions of HoK videos), tieba.baidu.com HoK section, NGA 王者荣耀 zone
- Zhihu: Strategy and personality/archetype discussions popular
- Korea: 왕자영요 search term, Naver cafe, YouTube Korean content (via Level Infinite KR push)
- Reddit: r/HonorOfKings (smaller English-speaking international community)
- Websites with active zh quizzes: youxl.cn, psyctest.cn, arealme.com/king-of-glory (confirmed via WebSearch)

- psyctest.cn — "哪个王者荣耀英雄最像你" (hero similarity, not player psychology)
- arealme.com — "你的性格最适合王者荣耀的哪个英雄" (hero recommendation)
- licece.com — "测测你的王者荣耀本命英雄"
- Source: WebSearch results (May 2026)

**Gap:** All existing zh tests match the player to a **hero**, not to a **player behavioral archetype**. SBTI's approach (what kind of *player behavior* are you, not which hero) is a genuine content gap. The archetype names like "野区税务稽查员" (Jungle Tax Auditor) and "嘲讽信号市长" (BM Ping Mayor) have no equivalent in existing quizzes.

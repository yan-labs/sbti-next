# VALORANT — SEO / GEO Research

---

## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global, with strength in NA, Korea, Japan, and SEA. PC bang Korea: **7.44% of PC bang play time** (June 2025, Gametrics/KOCCA, Source: https://welcon.kocca.kr). Twitch: **42.38M viewer hours** (May 2026, TwitchMetrics, Source: https://www.twitchmetrics.net/games/viewership). Riot operates VCT (VALORANT Champions Tour) with 4 international leagues. Japanese community is notably strong for a non-JP game — ZETA DIVISION (Japanese team) won VALORANT Champions 2022 and built massive domestic following.

**Dominant languages:** English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese. All 4 SBTI languages are highly relevant.

**Key communities:**
- Reddit: r/VALORANT (~1.5M members)
- Discord: Official Valorant server; team-specific servers
- Japan: note.com creator posts (character 診断 format is popular), ShinDanMaker, diagnose.me platforms; ZETA/team fandoms
- Korea: Inven VALORANT section, dak.gg community, fmkorea
- China: Bilibili VALORANT channels; less dominant than LoL/HoK but real
- Twitch: TenZ, Asuna, S0m, Aspas — large followings; VCT broadcasts

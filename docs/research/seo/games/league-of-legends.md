# League of Legends — SEO / GEO Research

---

## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** Global. PC bang (Korea) is dominant: LoL held **33.56% of PC bang play time** in June 2025, far ahead of any other title. Source: https://welcon.kocca.kr (KOCCA/Gametrics data). China (LPL) and Southeast Asia (VCS/PCS) are major markets. US/EU via LCS/LEC.

**Dominant languages on SBTI for this game:** Korean (PC bang driven), Chinese (LPL + Tencent ecosystem), English. Japanese audience exists but is smaller than for Valorant.

**Key communities:**
- Reddit: r/leagueoflegends (~7M members), r/leagueofmemes
- Discord: Official LoL server, role-specific servers (e.g., r/supportlol Discord)
- China: NGA (nga.178.com) LoL section, Bilibili LoL content creators, QQ LoL communities
- Korea: Inven (inven.co.kr) — the primary KR LoL forum; DCInside LoL galley
- Japan: 4gamer.net LoL section (smaller community)
- Twitter/X: LCK, LPL official accounts; players like Faker, Ruler, BeryL active

- SERP is almost entirely quiz-farm pages with DA < 30

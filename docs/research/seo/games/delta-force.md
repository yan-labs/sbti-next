## 1. Audience Snapshot

**Geographic distribution:** PC-dominant, China-primary, with growing global reach. Steam players: **~90,272 live / 247,028 all-time peak** on 2025-09-26 (SteamDB, Source: https://steamdb.info/app/2507950/charts/). Steam stats 2026-05-11: **94,688 current / 174,913 daily peak** (Source: https://store.steampowered.com/stats/stats/?l=english). Developed by Team Jade / TiMi Studio Group (Tencent). Available globally on PC and console since late 2024. Xbox Wire published an article about the game's extraction mode update in February 2026 (Source: https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/02/03/delta-force-become-the-boss/).

**Dominant languages:** Chinese (zh) is the largest segment by player count; English (global Steam players); the game has meaningful Western uptake given its free-to-play AAA quality.

**Key communities:**
- Official site: playdeltaforce.com
- Steam community hub: store.steampowered.com/app/2507950
- Reddit: r/DeltaForceGame (smaller, growing)
- Bilibili: Major CN content hub for Delta Force
- Chinese: Tieba, NGA Delta Force section
- PCGamesN — English press coverage: https://www.pcgamesn.com/delta-force-hawk-ops/operators

- No dedicated "Delta Force game personality quiz" pages exist in top results as of May 2026
- Source: WebSearch results (May 2026)

**Opportunity:** SBTI would be the first real quiz for Delta Force player types in en and zh. First-mover with structured data, 30 questions, and proper localization faces essentially no competition.

**Risk:** Ambiguity with the military unit — titles and meta should always say "Delta Force game" or "Delta Force player quiz" not just "Delta Force quiz" which could confuse intent.
